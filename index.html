Bitcoin Market Cycles Charts

The bitcoin price history to date has proven to be highly cyclical, with these bull and bear market periods repeating every halving, an event that occurs every 210000 blocks, which occurs approximately every four years.

Here we leave a series of graphs to try to identify these cycles.

Bitcoin Price USD

The price of BTC throughout its history

Bitcoin Realized and Delta Price

It's the value of all coins at the price they were bought divided by the amount of BTC that is in circulation

Bitcoin Halving Cycle

The evolution of the price of BTC in the 3 halvings is compared

50 and 200 day moving average

The moving average of 50 and 200 days are calculated by adding the last 50 or 200 values and then dividing the result

Influence of the halving on the monthly price

Each bar corresponds to the percentage of rise or fall of BTC in a month during the halving cycles

Bitcoin price halving radar chart

A complete turn corresponds to 4 years, which is approximately when the halving occurs

200 week moving average heatmap

This indicator uses a colored heat map based on percentage increases from that 200-week moving average

Bitcoin Fear and Greed heatmap

Identify the extent to which the market is becoming too fearful or too greedy

Bitcoin SOPR 7d moving average

Spent Output Profit Ratio (SOPR) is calculated by dividing the price sold by the price paid in USD
