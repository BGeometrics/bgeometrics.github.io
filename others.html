Bitcoin Others Charts

This page includes bitcoin charts that do not fall into the other categories.

Exchange Reserve

Total BTC held on exchanges. A large number of coins in the addresses of the exchanges indicates high selling pressure and leads to a price drop

Bitcoin Fear and Greed

Fear and Greed Index is a sentiment analysis that tries to measure the general sentiment of Bitcoin

Bitcoin Price Day

BTC price a day like today

Bitcoin Volatility

How the price of BTC fluctuates

Bitcoin Dominance

Proportion of the total cryptocurrency market that is represented by the market value of Bitcoin

Bitcoin Change Held on Exchanges

BTC rises on exchanges when more investors prefer to sell rather than buy

Bitcoin Exchange Net Position

Bitcoin Change Reserver Exchanges

Bitcoin Exchange Netflow

The difference between the coins entering and leaving the exchanges. Netflow = Inflow - Outflow

Bitcoin Exchange Inflow

The total amount of BTC transferred to the exchange

Bitcoin Miner Reserve

Coins held by miners' wallets

Bitcoin Miner Pool

The destination of the BTC of the mining pools

Bitcoin CDD (Coin Days Destroyed)

CDD is calculated by multiplying the amount of Bitcoin transferred by the number of days since BTC was last added to an address. Provides long-term sentiment and behavior for holders

Bitcoin CDD Adjusted 90d MA

Bitcoin Days Destroyed (BDD) indicates a distribution of ancient coins by long-term holders. All previous market spikes have been accompanied by a period of high destruction of old coins, so the metric can be used to capture the late stages of bullish cycles

Bitcoin Daily Price Performance

BTC price change percentage in one day

Bitcoin Puell Multiple

Shows market cycles from a mining revenue perspective

200 week moving average heatmap

This indicator uses a colored heat map based on percentage increases from that 200-week moving average

LTH vs STH

Long Term Hodler supply vs Short Term Hodler supply

Bitcoin Liveliness

It is the ratio between the sum of the days of coins destroyed (CDD) and the sum of all the days of coins created. It increases as holders liquidate positions and decreases while hodl

Stablecoin Supply Ratio (SSR)

It is the relationship between the supply of Bitcoin and the supply of stablecoins. When the SSR is high there is an uptrend for BTC

BTC Price Open, High, Low and Close
