Bitcoin DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging)

Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA) in Bitcoin is a periodic and constant way of investing.

This method of investing in BTC is based on distributing the investment into recurring purchases to balance purchases made at high and low prices.

DCA is a popular technique among Bitcoin investors because it minimizes market volatility and promotes sustained long-term portfolio growth.
