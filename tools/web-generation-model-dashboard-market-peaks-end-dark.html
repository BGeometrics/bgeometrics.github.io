Dashboard Bitcoin Identify Market Peaks

On-chain metrics to identify potential market peaks and end of bullish cycle in BTC.

Value Days Destroyed (VDD) Multiple Value is calculated by multiplying Coin Days Destroyed by the price of BTC.
High values occur when older coins are being sold, indicating that long-term holders want to take profits and this usually indicates that the bull run is coming to an end.

NUPL (Net Unrealized Profit Loss)
It is an indicator used to assess the aggregate profitability of all Bitcoin holders in the market.
It measures the difference between the net unrealized profit or loss of the coins in circulation and helps to identify the prevailing market sentiment (such as optimism, euphoria, anxiety, or capitulation).

NRPL (Net Realized Profit/Loss)
Measures the net realized profit or loss of all Bitcoin transactions occurring on the network.
- When NRPL is positive investors are making profits on the transactions they make. This may indicate that the market is in an optimistic phase.
- When NRPL is negative, it indicates that investors are selling at a loss. This could happen in periods of price correction or bear markets, where investors sell their assets in panic or to minimize their losses.

The MVRV Z-Score
Measures the deviation between Bitcoin's market capitalization (Market Value) and realized capitalization (Realized Value).
It is used to identify when the price of Bitcoin is significantly above or below its fair value, as defined by its realized capitalization.

Hodl Waves. LTH
The percentage of bitcoin that is held by LTH (Long-Term Holders) which refers to BTC that has not been moved/spent/sold for more than 6 months.

The Pi Cycle Indicator
It is a technical indicator designed to identify market peaks in the Bitcoin price cycle.
It was created by Philip Swift and uses two moving averages of specific periods that have historically accurately pinpointed the tops of Bitcoin's market cycles.

MVOCDD: Median Value of Coin Days Destroyed
When VOCDD and MVOCDD exceed the price of BTC, it usually indicates profit-taking by long-term investors and may mean a possible price pullback.

Realized Profit Loss Ratio
It is a metric used to assess market sentiment by analyzing whether participants who are selling are doing so at a profit or at a loss.

Bitcoin Moving Average A Bitcoin price moving average chart combines Bitcoin price data with a 111-day moving average which is the best performing moving average for BTC over time.

BGeometrics Market Peaks Index
The chart with the historical data of the Market Peaks Index along with the price of bitcoin.

Speedmeter Market Peaks
Indicates how far we are from a market peak in the price of BTC.
If the value is close to 100% we are closer to a market top for bitcoin
If the value is close to 0% it does not indicate that we are far from the market top.
