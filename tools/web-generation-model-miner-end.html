Bitcoin Miner Dashboard

Bitcoin mining charts display data and trends related to the mining process.

Mining in Bitcoin is the process by which transactions are validated and recorded on the blockchain, by performing SHA256 on transactions.

These charts provide insight into the health, security, and sustainability of the network.

Miners use this data to optimize their operations and make informed decisions about when and how to mine, and investors can use these charts to assess the state of the network and make predictions about the future price of Bitcoin.
