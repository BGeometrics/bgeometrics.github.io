Bitcoin Hodl Waves

Bitcoin HODL waves are a visual representation of the distribution of Bitcoin holdings based on how long they have remained unmoved.

Each colored band on a HODL waves chart indicates the percentage of the total Bitcoin supply that was last transacted within a specific time period, ranging from less than a day to over ten years.

This visualization provides insight into the holding behaviors of Bitcoin owners and can signal changes in market sentiment.
