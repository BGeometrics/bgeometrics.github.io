Bitcoin Terminal Price

It is calculated by adding the product of the Coin Days Destroyed (CDD) times the price divided by the multiplication of the number of days elapsed by the BTC in circulation.

CDD measures transaction activity by considering the time a Bitcoin has been inactive before moving.
Each Bitcoin (or fraction) accumulates one day of age for each day it does not move.
