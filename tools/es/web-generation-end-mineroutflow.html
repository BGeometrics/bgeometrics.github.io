Bitcoin Miner Outflow

It is the total amount of bitcoin transferred from miners' addresses to other addresses.

Miners accumulate BTC and miners' bitcoin outflows show valuable patterns in how they manage their bitcoins.

Bitcoin outflows from miners' addresses can indicate that they are transferring them to exchanges to sell.
