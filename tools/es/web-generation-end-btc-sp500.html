BTC, S&P 500, Gold, Tesla and MicroStrategy

Comparison of prices since 2015 of bitcoin, S&P 500, gold, Tesla and MicroStratety.

  • Bitcoin

    BTC is the financial asset with the best performance in the last 10 years.

  • S&P 500

    The S&P 500, an index comprised of 500 of the largest U.S. companies weighted by market capitalization, is a critical benchmark for investors around the world. The performance of the S&P 500 reflects the overall health of the stock market and economic conditions in the United States.
    The S&P 500 throughout its history averages approximately a 10% annual gain.
    To date, bitcoin has a high correlation with the S&P 500 index.

  • Gold

    Gold is the historical store of value.
    Gold has long been a safe haven in times of economic uncertainty and market volatility. Its inherent value, durability, and relative scarcity have made it a desired asset throughout history.

  • Tesla

    Tesla is the disruption in the automotive sector.
    Its focus on electric vehicles, renewable energy and battery technology has captured the attention of investors and consumers alike.

  • MicroStrategy

    MicroStrategy bets on bitcoin.
    It is a business intelligence company, it has adopted a strategy of converting a significant part of its treasury into Bitcoin.

  • In conclusion

    Bitcoin, the S&P 500, gold, Tesla and MicroStrategy represent a wide range of assets with different risk profiles and return potential. While all of these assets have experienced price fluctuations, their fundamental drivers and role in investment portfolios are different.
