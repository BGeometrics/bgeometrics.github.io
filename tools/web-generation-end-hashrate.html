Bitcoin Hash Rate

The Hashrate or hash rate of Bitcoin is a metric related to the amount of computing that occurs throughout the network in the process of mining Bitcoin.

The greater the hash (computing) power of the network, the greater its security and overall resistance to attacks.

The exact hash power of Bitcoin is not known, it is estimated from the number of blocks being mined and the current difficulty of the block.
